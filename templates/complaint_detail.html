Ref: #SC-90421 IN PROGRESS

Drinking Water Supply Interruption

Submitted by Rajesh K. Hamal • Kathmandu, Ward No. 12

Issue Description

The drinking water supply in the Sundhara area has been completely interrupted for the past 48 hours. This has caused significant distress to approximately 150 households in the immediate vicinity. No prior notice was issued by the Water Authority regarding maintenance or scheduled downtime.

We have attempted to contact the local ward office, but have not received a definitive timeline for the restoration of service. This is a critical utility matter requiring immediate attention from the municipal technical team.
